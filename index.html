About

Presley Chweneyagae is a South African actor best known for his lead role in the 2005 Oscar-winning film Tsotsi. Born on October 19, 1984, he has had an impactful career in film and theatre.

He began acting in community theatre and rose to fame with *Tsotsi*, earning international recognition.

Achievements

  • 2005: Starred in Tsotsi (Academy Award Winner for Best Foreign Language Film)
  • TV: Featured in popular South African series like *The River* and *Gomora*
  • Theatre: Strong advocate for social issues through stage performance
